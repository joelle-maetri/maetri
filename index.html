WHAT ARE EARLY ALERTS

Early alerts are pattern-based signals of change.

Each on its own might mean little. But together, they point to something forming beneath the surface.

Maetri recognizes these signals—and prompts action while there's still time.

They are subtle:

  • A pause in speech
  • A shorter stride
  • A missed meal
  • A shift in expression
  • A second "what?" in a row

These are not yet emergencies. But they're the beginning of patterns Maetri is trained to see.
